Privacy Policy

Last updated: 30 Dec 2025

Klippod ("we", "our", "us") is a mobile app for clipping and rendering videos locally on your device. This policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights.

What We Collect

  • Local media: Videos you select for clipping stay on your device. We do not upload raw video.
  • Optional cloud processing: If you enable subtitle/hook detection, we extract relevant audio segments and send them to Groq for transcription and AI suggestions.
  • API keys: Your Groq API key is stored securely on-device via encrypted storage and is not transmitted to us.
  • Diagnostics: We may log non-personal crash/usage data locally for troubleshooting; nothing is sent to our servers.

How We Use Data

  • Render clips on-device according to your settings.
  • Send extracted audio to Groq only when subtitle/hook features are enabled.
  • Store your preferences (layout, overlay, ratios) locally for convenience.

Data Sharing

  • Groq: Receives the audio excerpt you submit for transcription and AI hook suggestions. Groq processes this to return text and suggested clip ranges.
  • No other third parties receive your content from us.

Retention

  • Media and renders remain on your device until you delete them.
  • Preferences (including API key) stay locally stored and can be cleared by reinstalling the app.
  • Audio sent to Groq is subject to Groq’s retention policies. We do not control or store Groq’s copies.

Your Choices & Controls

  • Disable subtitles/hook detection to avoid sending audio to Groq.
  • Revoke app permissions (media/storage) via system settings.
  • Clear the Groq API key in-app or by reinstalling to remove local data.

Permissions We Request

  • Media/Storage: To read your selected video and save rendered clips to your gallery.
  • Network: Only for Groq requests when subtitle/hook features are enabled.

Children

Klippod is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal data from children.

Changes

We may update this policy. Material changes will be reflected by updating the “Last updated” date.
